Comprehensive Painting Services

Everything your home needs, from a single room refresh to a full exterior transformation. House painting is our specialty, not a side job.

🏠
Exterior Painting
Seattle-area weather demands more than most homeowners realize. Moisture, temperature swings, and seasonal sun can reveal weak prep fast. Peeling and early fading happen when corners are cut. We don't cut corners.
  • Siding, trim & fascia
  • Fencing & decking
  • Surface prep prioritized
  • Weather-resistant coatings
🖼
Interior Painting
If your walls look scuffed, patchy, or dated, interior painting is one of the most effective ways to make your home feel fresh and updated. We protect floors and furniture, keep the site clean, and deliver crisp, intentional finishes.
  • Walls, ceilings & trim
  • Daily cleanup included
  • Color consultation free
  • Perfect edges, smooth finish
🪵
Cabinet Refinishing
You don't need to spend a fortune replacing cabinetry. Cabinet refinishing gives you a dramatic update without the remodel cost or the weeks of disruption. Proper prep, bonding primers, and durable topcoats that last.
  • Kitchen & bathroom cabinets
  • Proper degreasing & sanding
  • Showroom-smooth finish
  • No brush marks or orange peel
🌿
Deck Refinishing
Restore and protect your deck through every Pacific Northwest season. We strip, prep, and apply coatings built for real outdoor exposure.
  • Cleaning & stripping
  • Staining & sealing
  • UV & moisture protection
🔧
Drywall Repair
Seamless patches for cracks, holes, and water damage, finished so precisely you will never know they were there. Paint-ready surfaces, every time.
  • Crack & hole repair
  • Texture matching
  • Paint-ready finish
✨
Popcorn Ceiling Removal
Smooth out dated ceilings for a clean, modern look. We handle the dust, the mess, and the finish. The result is a clean, modern ceiling ready for paint.
  • Safe removal process
  • Smooth skim coat finish
  • Ready for paint same day
🏢
Commercial Painting
Your business should look professional and welcoming. We plan around your hours, keep the site clean, and deliver a crisp finish without disrupting operations.
  • Offices, retail & studios
  • Schedule-friendly crews
  • Fast, professional finish
🎨
Color Consultation
Not sure what colors will work best? We bring Benjamin Moore and Sherwin-Williams samples directly to your home so you can see how each color looks in your actual light.
  • Free with every project
  • Samples brought to you
  • Expert recommendations
