Frequently Asked Questions

Choosing a painting contractor does not have to feel like a risk. Here are honest answers to the questions homeowners ask us most.

❓How much does a free estimate cost?▾
Our estimates are completely free with no obligation. We visit your home, inspect every surface, review your options, and provide a detailed written proposal within 24 hours. You can also book your estimate appointment directly online. Call (425) 725-8134 to get started.
❓What does your 3-year warranty cover?▾
Our 3 year workmanship warranty covers peeling, bubbling, and defects caused by our application. Our bid is also final: if the scope stays the same, so does the price. This is one of the strongest written guarantees offered by any painter in Snohomish or King County.
❓Why is exterior painting in the Seattle area different?▾
Seattle's moisture, temperature swings, and seasonal sun exposure can reveal weak prep fast. Peeling, bubbling, and early fading are common when corners are cut. We prioritize thorough surface prep, proper product selection, and application methods that help coatings bond and perform through real Pacific Northwest seasons.
❓Do you offer financing options?▾
Yes, we offer flexible financing options to make your project affordable. Painting is an investment in your home's value and curb appeal, and we do not want budget concerns to hold you back. Ask us about financing when you request your free estimate.
