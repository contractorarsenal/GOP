Frequently Asked Questions

Choosing a painting contractor does not have to feel like a risk. Here are honest answers to the questions homeowners ask us most.

❓How much does a free estimate cost?▾
Our estimates are completely free with no obligation. We visit your home, inspect every surface, review your options, and provide a detailed written proposal within 24 hours. You can also book your estimate appointment directly online. Call (425) 725-8134 to get started.
❓What should a painting estimate include?▾
A good painting estimate should include: a clear scope of work, specific paint products and sheen levels, surface prep details, number of coats, timeline, and total price with no hidden fees. Our written proposals cover all of this so you know exactly what you're getting before we start.
❓What does your 3-year warranty cover?▾
Our 3 year workmanship warranty covers peeling, bubbling, and defects caused by our application. Our bid is also final: if the scope stays the same, so does the price. This is one of the strongest written guarantees offered by any painter in Snohomish or King County.
❓Why is exterior painting in the Seattle area different?▾
Seattle's moisture, temperature swings, and seasonal sun exposure can reveal weak prep fast. Peeling, bubbling, and early fading are common when corners are cut. We prioritize thorough surface prep, proper product selection, and application methods that help coatings bond and perform through real Pacific Northwest seasons.
❓How long does interior painting usually take?▾
A single room typically takes 1 to 2 days. A full interior can take 3 to 7 days depending on home size, ceiling height, and prep requirements. We will give you a firm timeline during your free estimate, and we stick to it with daily updates along the way.
❓Do you offer financing options?▾
Yes, we offer flexible financing options to make your project affordable. Painting is an investment in your home's value and curb appeal, and we do not want budget concerns to hold you back. Ask us about financing when you request your free estimate.
❓How durable is cabinet refinishing vs. replacing?▾
When done correctly, cabinet refinishing is extremely durable and costs a fraction of replacement, often 70 to 80% less. The key is proper degreasing, sanding, bonding primer, and a durable topcoat. We focus on every step of prep so your cabinets look smooth, modern, and refined for years.
❓How do I choose the right colors for my home?▾
Our free color consultation handles exactly that. We bring Benjamin Moore and Sherwin-Williams samples directly to your home so you can see how colors look in your actual lighting, inside and outside. We will also give you professional guidance on what tends to work well with your home's architecture and neighborhood.
❓Are you EPA Lead-Safe Certified?▾
Yes. Golden Oak Painting is EPA Lead Safe Certified, which is especially important for homes built before 1978. We follow all required safety protocols for disturbing lead-containing paint surfaces, protecting your family, our crew, and the environment.
❓How do you protect my home during painting?▾
We cover floors and furniture, mask all trim and fixtures, protect landscaping and hardscapes, and do daily cleanup at the end of each work day. Your home is treated with the same care and respect we would give our own. We do not leave a mess for you to deal with.
❓When is the best time to book painters?▾
For exterior work, late spring through early fall gives the best conditions. Interior work can happen year-round. Some of our best interior projects happen in fall and winter. Our schedule fills quickly in summer, so we recommend requesting your free estimate as early as possible to secure your preferred dates.
❓How fast can you start my project?▾
We prioritize fast scheduling. Reach out today and we will typically have your free estimate within 24 to 48 hours. Project start times vary by season and current schedule, but we always do our best to accommodate timing that matters to you. Just let us know your target dates when you call.
