Frequently Asked Questions

Choosing a painting contractor shouldn't feel like a gamble. Here are honest answers to the questions homeowners ask us most.

❓How much does a free estimate cost?▾
Nothing at all. Our estimates are completely free with no obligation. We visit your home, inspect every surface, review your options, and provide a detailed written proposal within 24 hours. You can also book your estimate appointment directly online. Call (425) 725-8134 to get started.
❓What should a painting estimate include?▾
A good painting estimate should include: a clear scope of work, specific paint products and sheen levels, surface prep details, number of coats, timeline, and total price with no hidden fees. Our written proposals cover all of this so you know exactly what you're getting before we start.
❓What does your 3 year warranty cover?▾
Our 3 year workmanship warranty covers peeling, bubbling, and defects caused by our application. Our bid is also final: if the scope stays the same, so does the price. This is one of the strongest written guarantees offered by any painter in Snohomish or King County.
❓Why is exterior painting in the Seattle area different?▾
Seattle's moisture, temperature swings, and seasonal sun exposure can reveal weak prep quickly. Peeling, bubbling, and early fading are common when corners are cut. We prioritize thorough surface prep, proper product selection, and application methods that help coatings bond and perform through real Pacific Northwest seasons.
❓How long does interior painting usually take?▾
A single room typically takes 1 to 2 days. A full interior can take 3 to 7 days depending on home size, ceiling height, and prep requirements. We'll give you a firm timeline during your free estimate, and we adhere to it with daily updates throughout the project.
❓Do you offer financing options?▾
Yes, we offer flexible financing options to make your project accessible. Painting is an investment in your home's value and curb appeal, and we don't want budget concerns to hold you back. Ask us about financing when you request your free estimate.
❓How durable is cabinet refinishing vs. replacing?▾
When done correctly, cabinet refinishing is extremely durable and costs a fraction of replacement, often 60 to 80 percent less. The key is proper degreasing, sanding, bonding primer, and a durable topcoat. We focus on every step of prep so your cabinets look smooth, modern, and refined for years.
❓How do I choose the right colors for my home?▾
That's what our free color consultation is for. We bring Benjamin Moore and Sherwin-Williams samples directly to your home so you can see how colors look in your actual lighting, both indoors and outdoors. We'll also give you professional guidance on what tends to work well with your home's architecture and neighborhood.
❓Are you EPA Lead Safe Certified?▾
Yes. Golden Oak Painting is EPA Lead Safe Certified, which is especially important for homes built before 1978. We follow all required safety protocols for disturbing lead-containing paint surfaces, protecting your family, our crew, and the environment.
❓How do you protect my home during painting?▾
We cover floors and furniture, mask all trim and fixtures, protect landscaping and hardscapes, and do daily cleanup at the end of each work day. Your home is treated with the same care and respect we'd give our own. We don't leave a mess for you to deal with.
❓When is the best time to book painters?▾
For exterior work, late spring through early fall gives the best conditions. Interior work can happen year-round. Some of our finest interior projects are completed in fall and winter. That said, our schedule fills quickly in summer, so we recommend requesting your free estimate as early as possible to secure your preferred dates.
❓How fast can you start my project?▾
We prioritize fast scheduling. Reach out today and we will typically have your free estimate within 24 to 48 hours. Project start times vary by season and current schedule, but we always do our best to accommodate timing that matters to you. Just let us know your target dates when you call.

Our Proven Process

What Happens Day 1 Through Done

Most painters show up and start rolling. We don't. Here's the rigorous step-by-step process we follow on every single project.

1
Free On Site Walkthrough & Written Proposal
We visit your home, inspect every surface, note any damage or rot, and walk you through your options. Within 24 hours you receive a detailed, transparent written proposal covering scope, products, timeline, and final price. No vague estimates.
2
Color Consultation: Samples Brought to Your Home
After booking, we schedule a free color consultation and bring Benjamin Moore and Sherwin-Williams samples directly to your home so you can see exactly how colors look in your specific lighting, morning, afternoon, and evening. No more guessing from a tiny chip at the store.
3
Full Site Protection Before a Drop of Paint
Day one: floors covered, furniture moved and wrapped, landscaping and hardscaping protected, trim and fixtures masked. We treat your home like a professional job site: organized, clean, and respectful of everything in it.
4
Meticulous Surface Prep: The Step Others Skip
This is where the job is won or lost. We clean, degrease, sand, scrape failed coatings, fill cracks and holes, caulk gaps, and apply the right primer for each surface. Proper prep is why our paint jobs hold up for years while others peel in months.
5
Expert Application: Premium Products, Perfect Finish
We apply premium Sherwin-Williams and Benjamin Moore coatings using professional techniques for each surface type: cut-in edges on interior trim, back-rolling exterior siding, spraying cabinets for a factory-smooth finish. Clean lines. No drips. No missed spots.
6
Daily Cleanup & Proactive Communication
At the end of every workday we clean up the site so your home stays livable throughout the project. We also proactively update you on progress, next steps, and any changes so you're never left wondering what's happening.
7
Final Walkthrough + 3 Year Written Warranty
When the work is complete, we do a thorough walkthrough with you, room by room and surface by surface. We address any touch-ups on the spot. Once you're fully happy, we leave your home spotless and hand you your 3 year written workmanship warranty.
